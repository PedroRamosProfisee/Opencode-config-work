# Step 01 — Intake

## Goal

Establish what is being grilled, which mode to use, whether artifacts should be written, and what assumptions are allowed.

## Required first moves

1. Give a TL;DR of how Deep Grilled will proceed.
2. Run the Mode AUQ from `SKILL.md` unless the user already specified a mode.
3. Ask whether artifacts should be summarized only or written to `.deep_grilled/`.
4. Confirm indie/SaaS defaults for market analysis.
5. Start a decision ledger with known facts, assumptions, and open branches.

## Intake questions

Ask only the missing items:

1. What is the idea/plan/project in one paragraph?
2. What decision are we trying to make?
3. Is the goal to validate, design, monetize, implement, or kill the idea?
4. Who is the intended user?
5. Who is the buyer?
6. What stage is this in: idea, prototype, shipped, revenue, scaling?
7. What constraints matter: time, money, tech stack, distribution, compliance, team size?
8. If this is a personal/internal tool, is personal usefulness itself a win?

## Recommended defaults

- Mode: **Standard**.
- Artifact behavior: **summarize first, write only after confirmation**.
- Market frame: **indie/SaaS, founder-led, low-budget, validation before build**.
- Tone: **supportive, direct, recommendations included**.
- For personal/family-origin ideas: **family-first/personal-first MVP with commercialization evidence built in**.
