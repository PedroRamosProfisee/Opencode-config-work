# Step 03 — Foundation

## Goal

Clarify the core job: what is the goal, who it is for, why now, and what success means.

## Question branches

Resolve these in order:

1. **Goal** — What is the goal, and how do we get there?
2. **User** — Who experiences the pain?
3. **Buyer** — Who pays or approves purchase?
4. **Pain** — What painful, frequent, urgent problem exists?
5. **Alternatives** — What do they do today instead?
6. **Wedge** — Why would this win despite existing options?
7. **Constraints** — What must be true for this to work?
8. **Non-goals** — What should this explicitly not do?

## Recommended framing

For indie/SaaS ideas, recommend narrow wedge over broad platform:

```text
Serve one reachable audience with one painful workflow and one clear outcome before expanding.
```

## Smell checks

Flag these kindly but directly:

- “Everyone” as the user.
- Buyer and user are different but not acknowledged.
- Problem is interesting but not urgent.
- Product is described by features, not outcome.
- “AI wrapper” with no workflow ownership.
- No named alternative.
- No path to first 10 users.
