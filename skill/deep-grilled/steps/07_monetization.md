# Step 07 — Monetization

## Goal

Determine whether the idea can make money with a plausible pricing model, buyer, and unit economics.

## Branches to resolve

1. **Buyer vs user** — Who pays and who uses?
2. **Budget source** — Which existing budget does this come from?
3. **Value metric** — Per seat, per project, per workflow, per usage, per outcome?
4. **Pricing anchor** — What do alternatives cost?
5. **Willingness to pay** — What outcome justifies the price?
6. **Packaging** — Free trial, freemium, paid pilot, subscription, usage-based, service-assisted.
7. **Gross margin** — Model/API costs, hosting, support, manual operations.
8. **CAC path** — How expensive is it to acquire one customer?
9. **Retention driver** — What causes recurring use or renewal?
10. **Expansion path** — Higher tier, team seats, integrations, usage, services.

## Recommended indie/SaaS defaults

- Prefer **paid pilot or paid beta** over free waitlist when B2B pain is credible.
- Prefer **simple monthly subscription** unless usage cost varies wildly.
- Avoid freemium unless distribution is strong and marginal cost is low.
- Start with a price that creates signal, not vanity adoption.
- Define one value metric customers understand.
